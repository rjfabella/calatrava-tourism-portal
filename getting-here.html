Getting to Calatrava

Calatrava sits on the northernmost tip of Tablas Island — the largest island in Romblon. Getting here is part of the adventure.

By Air: Clark → Tugdan Airport (Tablas) — ~1 hr flight
By Sea: Batangas or Lucena Port → Tablas — 6–12 hrs
From Tugdan Airport to Calatrava: ~2 hours by road

Need help planning your trip?

The Tourism Council can assist with transport arrangements, guide bookings, and local information.
